Who we are

Philippine Canada Immigration Consultancy (PCIC) was established in Calgary, Alberta by group of individuals who started as an International Student that are now successful in their field of expertise.

We at PCIC strived to be one of the leading immigration consultancy that will help Filipinos to successfully achieve their dreams of studying in Canada while working. We are committed to deliver only the highest quality of service to our clients to be with them in each step of the way, from lodging the application until their application to being a permamnent resident.
